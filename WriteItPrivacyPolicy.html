Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 09, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and handled when you use the Write It! application.

1. Data Collection and Storage

We strongly believe in your right to privacy. All data you enter into Write It!, including your habits, tasks, and completion logs, is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, harvest, transmit, or store your personal data on any external developer servers.

2. iCloud Synchronization

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, your app data may be securely synced across your Apple devices using your personal iCloud account. This synchronization process is handled entirely by Apple's native frameworks and is governed by Apple's privacy policy. We do not have direct access to your iCloud data.

3. Third-Party Services

We respect your focus and privacy. The application does not integrate any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising trackers, or external data collection tools. Your daily routines and productivity statistics remain completely private.

4. Widget and System Integrations

When you use Home Screen widgets or interact with the app via Siri and Apple Intelligence, the app only accesses your local database to display or update your tasks. This interaction happens strictly on your device.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
